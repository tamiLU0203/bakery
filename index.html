Tartine at home

In 2010 we published TARTINE BREAD. The baking community was in shock that we would share our secret to the iconic Tartine Country Loaf. For us, the magic is in the endless thought and experimentation that goes into making each loaf; learning to push the boundaries of what we do, so more people can enjoy it.

OUR PARTNER

Coffee Manufactory

Customers at Tartine Bakery came to us for the highest quality bread and pastry, and now we can confidently say our coffee program matches. Together we are building Coffee Manufactory. Developed with the same commitment towards sustainable sourcing and farming relationships we have with our grain partners.

We want to bring customers closer to farms, farms closer to roasters, roasters closer to baristas, and back again. Back to the roots of simple beans, great farming friendships, and precise roasting. Back to being good, balanced, clean.
